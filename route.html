In Enrique's first attempt, he leaves Tegucigalpa, Honduras, and travels to the Mexican border town of Tapachula with the intention of entering Mexico. Enrique successfully crosses the Suchiate River into Mexico, but Mexican immigration officers detain him and send him back to Honduras.

Enrique reaches the Mexican city of Tijuana in the north on his second attempt, but he is attacked and assaulted by a gang of local thugs, and he is again sent back to Honduras.

Enrique begins his third effort to cross the border, crossing through Guatemala and Mexico while atop the "La Bestia" trains, which are frequently used by migrants. He is at danger of being attacked by bandits, falling from the train, and being detained by Mexican police, among other things.

Finally, Enrique arrives at the Mexican border town of Nuevo Laredo, from where he intends to enter the US. Enrique and the other immigrants cross the Rio Grande river into the country. Then, He was smuggled to Texas with some other group of smugglers, where they contacted his mom and asked for more money in order to send their son ahead. His mom, Lourdes agreed and send her boyfriend to pick up her son from Texas and bring him back to North Carolina
